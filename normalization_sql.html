Normalization is the process of organizing data in a database to reduce redundancy and improve data integrity. It breaks large tables into smaller ones and defines relationships between them.

-- Normalized
| student_id | student_name |
| student_id | subject       |

-- Normalized
| department | dept_location    |
| student_id | name | department |
